FAQ

Quick answers to common questions.

Do you work on Audi, VW, BMW, and Mercedes?

Yes. We specialize in European brands and have the tooling to diagnose them correctly.

Do you warranty your work?

Yes. We include a 12-month parts and labor warranty on qualifying repairs.

Why do you charge for diagnostics?

Proper diagnosis takes time, data review, and testing. It protects you from unnecessary parts replacement.

Can I bring my own parts?

We can discuss customer-supplied parts case by case to ensure fit and warranty coverage.

How do you handle approvals and estimates?

We provide a findings summary and clear options before any work begins.

How soon can you schedule?

Call or submit the form. We will confirm the next available appointment window.
